Our Story

Eighteen years of quietly obsessive dentistry.

Your Clinic Name started as a single chair in 2008. Today we're a full specialist practice — but the same rule still governs every appointment: explain first, treat second.

Milestones

How the clinic grew.

2008

A single-chair practice opens

Dr. Rao begins seeing patients from a two-room office, focused entirely on general dentistry.

2014

Cosmetic & implant wing added

A second specialist joins to build out veneers, whitening, and implant surgery.

2019

Digital smile design introduced

3D intraoral scanning replaces impressions across every treatment plan.

2026

Six specialists, one address

Orthodontics, pediatric care, and sedation dentistry all under one roof.
